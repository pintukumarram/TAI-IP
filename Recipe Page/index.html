South Indian Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 1/2 cup of lentils
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • Curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Spices (turmeric, chili powder)

Instructions

  1. Soak the rice and lentils for 2 hours.
  2. Grind to a smooth batter and ferment overnight.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds and curry leaves.
  4. Add chopped onions and tomatoes, sauté until soft.
  5. Pour the batter, add spices and salt, cook on medium flame.
  6. Serve hot with chutney or sambar.

North Indian Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 cup of yogurt
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • Fresh coriander
  • Salt to taste
  • Spices (garam masala, turmeric, chili powder)

Instructions

  1. Mix flour and yogurt to form a dough. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds.
  3. Add chopped onions and tomatoes, sauté until soft.
  4. Add spices and salt, cook until the oil separates.
  5. Roll the dough into small balls and flatten them.
  6. Cook on a hot griddle until golden brown.
  7. Serve hot with curry or dal.

Italian Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g of pasta
  • 1/2 cup of tomato sauce
  • 1 onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Fresh basil
  • Salt to taste
  • Grated cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté chopped garlic and onions.
  3. Add tomato sauce, basil, and salt. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Toss the cooked pasta with the sauce.
  5. Serve hot with grated cheese on top.
